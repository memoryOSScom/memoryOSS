**Why do we need a CLA?**

memoryOSS uses dual licensing:

- **Community:** AGPL-3.0 — free and open source, copyleft
- **Enterprise:** Commercial license — for organizations that cannot use AGPL

The CLA ensures we can offer both licenses without needing individual permission from every contributor. Without it, dual licensing would be legally impossible at scale.

You keep full credit and copyright for your work. The CLA simply grants us the flexibility to license the combined project under multiple terms.
